Immune Cell Knowledge Graph

An interactive tool for examination of immune cell types, offering pathway analysis, gene set discovery, and perturbation predictions in a cell type specific manner. All findings are backed by published literature with interactive visualizations.

"Reverse genetics is a method in molecular genetics that is used to help understand the function(s) of a gene by analysing the phenotypic effects caused by the alteration of the gene"

Output top 10 activated or inhibited genes/phenotypes by a gene of interest based on PageRank score.
